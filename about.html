ABOUT US

Founded in 2019, Norden Techlab (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. has been operating successfully in the field of material testing. Initially launched as a proprietorship, the company continues to thrive under this structure by offering quality testing solutions to clients across multiple industries.

Our CEO oversees all operations of the testing laboratory, which functions as a core department of Norden Technologies. The lab is staffed with highly qualified engineers, technicians, and professionals dedicated to providing reliable and accurate testing reports.

We continuously strive to uphold the highest standards in testing and validation, supported by a dedicated marketing and operations team.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to ensure trust and reliability in every testing process, supporting industries with accurate and timely testing data that meet international standards.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become the preferred global partner in the field of testing and validation, supporting clients in delivering exceptional products, services, and solutions.
